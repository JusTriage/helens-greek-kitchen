Our Story

Bringing Greece to Bedford

At Helen's Greek Kitchen, we bring the authentic taste of Greece to your table. Our kitchen celebrates the vibrant flavors of the Mediterranean, where every dish tells a story of Greek tradition and culinary passion.

Located at The Three Cups Pub on Newnham Street, we offer a convenient takeaway service for those craving genuine Greek cuisine. From our kitchen to your home, experience the warmth of Greek hospitality.
